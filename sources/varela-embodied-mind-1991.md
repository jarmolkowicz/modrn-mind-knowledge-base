# Varela, Thompson & Rosch (1991) - The Embodied Mind

## Citation

Varela, F. J., Thompson, E., & Rosch, E. (1991). *The Embodied Mind: Cognitive Science and Human Experience*. MIT Press. ISBN: 0262720213

**Link:** [MIT Press](https://mitpress.mit.edu/9780262720212/the-embodied-mind/)

## Type

Book (foundational theoretical)

## Key Insight

Cognition is fundamentally embodied—it emerges from having a body that interacts with the world. Knowledge isn't abstract information processing but arises from lived, sensory experience.

## Relevance

Establishes why AI cannot replace human insight that emerges from embodied experience. Your unique perspective comes from living in a body, in a context—something AI lacks entirely.
