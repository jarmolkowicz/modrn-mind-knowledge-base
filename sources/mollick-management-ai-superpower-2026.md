# Mollick (2026) - Management as AI Superpower

## Citation

Mollick, E. (2026). Management as AI superpower. *One Useful Thing*.

**URL:** https://www.oneusefulthing.org/p/management-as-ai-superpower

## Type

Article (Substack post, practitioner)

## Key Insight

Traditional management skills — scoping work, defining quality, delegating effectively — are the critical competitive advantage in an AI-driven workplace. "Knowing what to ask for" is the scarce resource, not execution capability. Domain expertise enables better instructions, faster evaluation, and more accurate quality assessment when working with AI agents.

## Relevance

Reinforces the KB's thesis that human judgment and domain expertise are irreplaceable. Provides a practical framework (equation of agentic work) for cost-benefit decisions about AI delegation. The MBA case study shows domain knowledge outperforms AI expertise — supporting the argument that capability preservation matters more than prompt skills.
