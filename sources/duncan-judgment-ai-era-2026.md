# Duncan (2026) - How Do Workers Develop Good Judgment in the AI Era?

## Citation

Duncan, D. S. (2026). How do workers develop good judgment in the AI era? *Harvard Business Review*.

**URL:** https://hbr.org/2026/02/how-do-workers-develop-good-judgment-in-the-ai-era

## Type

Article (practitioner, HBR)

## Key Insight

AI simultaneously increases the need for judgment and erodes the experiences that produce it. Traditional judgment development relied on repetitive, low-stakes tasks with real consequences — AI now generates these outputs instantly, eliminating the developmental struggle. "Human in the loop" doesn't solve this because escalation protocols train juniors to defer rather than decide.

## Relevance

Articulates the capacity-erosion thesis from an organizational/leadership angle. Introduces a five-type judgment taxonomy that gives educators concrete vocabulary. The paradox framing directly supports the KB's core argument about desirable difficulty and novice vulnerability.
