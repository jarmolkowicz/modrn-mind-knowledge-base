# Tsim & Gutoreva (2025) - SCAN Framework

## Citation

Tsim, F., & Gutoreva, A. (2025). SCAN: A Decision-Making Framework for Task Assignment with Generative AI. Preprint, December 2025.

**Status:** Preprint - not yet publicly indexed. Source PDF in local research folder.
**Verification:** Content verified from provided PDF; not findable via web search as of Feb 2026.

## Type

Paper (framework proposal)

## Key Insight

A human-centric framework for deciding when and how to involve AI in tasks, based on Vygotsky's Zone of Proximal Development and metacognition. Introduces four "sub-zones" (Substitute, Complement, Aid, Non-negotiable) determined by whether knowledge is "known to learner" and "known to GenAI."

## Relevance

Provides operational specificity for task-level decisions that Modern Mind's strategic alternation addresses at a higher level. Offers concrete criteria for when AI use leads to upskilling vs. deskilling.
