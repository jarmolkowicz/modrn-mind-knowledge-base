## [2026-04-30] ingest | chandra-sycophantic-delusional-2026 (Chandra, Kleiman-Weiner, Ragan-Kelley & Tenenbaum, MIT/UW; arXiv:2602.19141v1, Feb 2026) — Re-triggered from prior DEFER for Pass-3 batch. First formal computational model of how sycophancy causes delusional spiraling (catastrophic posterior drift toward a false hypothesis). 10,000-trial Monte Carlo simulations show even an idealized Bayes-rational user is vulnerable; sycophancy plays a causal role distinct from hallucination. Two intuitive mitigations reduce but do not eliminate the harm: factual-only output (a "factual sycophant" can spiral users via cherry-picked truths / "lies by omission") and user awareness (a sycophancy-informed level-3 Bayesian user remains vulnerable, an analogue to Kamenica & Gentzkow's Bayesian persuasion).
