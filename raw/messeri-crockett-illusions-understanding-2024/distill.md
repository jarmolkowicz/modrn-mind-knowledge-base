# Distillation: Messeri & Crockett (2024) — Illusions of Understanding in AI-Driven Scientific Research

## Extraction Ledger

### Concepts
- NEW: `illusion-of-explanatory-depth` — foundational construct (Rozenblit & Keil 2002) that has been *referenced inline* in `[[confidence-competence-gap]]` and `[[metacognition]]` but has never had a standalone entry. Messeri & Crockett anchor the construct to AI-in-science, making this the right moment to give it its own page. Status: solid (peer-reviewed, replicated in cog-psy literature for two decades, now applied in a Nature Perspective).
- NEW: `illusion-of-exploratory-breadth` — paper-original; describes the false belief that AI is exploring all testable hypotheses when it explores only AI-tractable ones. Not covered by any existing KB entry. Status: emerging (introduced in this paper; awaits empirical operationalization).
- NEW: `illusion-of-objectivity` — paper-original; describes the false belief that AI tools have no standpoint or represent all standpoints. Not covered by any existing KB entry. Status: emerging.
- NEW: `scientific-monoculture` — paper-original system-level construct; covers both monoculture-of-knowing and monoculture-of-knowers as paired sub-types. Distinct from `[[creativity-diversity-paradox]]` (output-level homogenization) and `[[leveling-effect]]` (individual capability convergence) — operates at the level of which questions get asked and which standpoints inform them. Status: emerging.
- UPDATE: `[[confidence-competence-gap]]` — extends the gap to community level; adds Messeri & Crockett as an additional source.
- UPDATE: `[[borrowed-certainty]]` — extends to scientific knowledge-production framing.
- UPDATE: `[[artificial-certainty]]` — Messeri & Crockett operate one nested level up from Leonardi & Leavell; same mechanism, different unit of analysis.
- UPDATE: `[[fluency-bias]]` — adds the four qualities (simple, broad, reductive, quantitative) and the field-scale structural consequence.
- UPDATE: `[[creativity-diversity-paradox]]` — extends from output-level to question-level homogenization.
- (no UPDATE to `[[metacognition]]`, `[[leveling-effect]]`, `[[novice-vulnerability]]` — already adequately covered; these are linked from the new entries instead.)

### Methods
- NEW: `ai-vision-taxonomy` — Oracle / Surrogate / Quant / Arbiter as a diagnostic framework for naming the role AI is being asked to play. Distinct from `[[partial-automation-principle]]` and `[[jagged-frontier]]`. Status: emerging.
