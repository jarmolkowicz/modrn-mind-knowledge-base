### Concepts

- NEW: `paradox-of-expertise` — labelled construct from Hermann et al. [p.5] for the pattern by which high-skilled workers overestimate their expertise, dismiss GenAI as unsuitable, and self-marginalize as lower-skilled colleagues build GenAI augmentation into workflows. Not currently in the KB under any name. Distinct from [[novice-vulnerability]] (mirror pattern at junior end) and [[upskilling-deskilling-paradox]] (within-individual dynamics during AI use).
- UPDATE: `professional-identity-threat` — embed in BPNT three-needs frame; add paradox-of-expertise and identity-threat-coping as related.
- UPDATE: `agency` — algorithmic cage, decoupled accountability, surveillance/monitoring as the most serious autonomy threats; workplace-deployment lens complementary to per-interaction situational disempowerment.
- UPDATE: `novice-vulnerability` — BPNT framing of simultaneous competence-satisfaction and competence-frustration for novices; pair with paradox-of-expertise as senior mirror pattern.
- UPDATE: `ai-self-efficacy-erosion` — comparison-threat and deskilling-by-offloading as competence-frustration mechanisms; ties to performance-paradox / cognitive-debt clusters.

### Methods

- NEW: `identity-threat-coping` — descriptive taxonomy of five strategies (direct resolution, symbolic self-completion, dissociation, escapism, fluid compensation) workers use to cope with GenAI-induced threats to basic psychological needs. Imported by Hermann et al. from self-discrepancy research and applied to GenAI workplace context. No direct counterpart in existing KB methods.

## Conservative Choices Documented

The triage flagged three potential NEW concepts:
1. **basic-psychological-needs-at-work** (BPNT triad as KB-level frame) — chose UPDATE over NEW. The BPNT framing is reasoning vocabulary the review supplies; promoting it to a standalone concept would duplicate Self-Determination Theory itself, which is well-established outside AI research. Better to thread BPNT into existing entries (professional-identity-threat, agency, ai-self-efficacy-erosion) as the integrating frame for the cluster, leaving room for a future dedicated entry only if a second source treats the BPNT triad as load-bearing for AI-specific work.
2. **identity-threat-coping** (five-strategy taxonomy) — chose NEW *method*. The five strategies form a structured framework with operational implications for organizational design. Genuinely new vocabulary for the KB; no existing method covers this. Status: emerging (theoretical framework; Hermann et al. provide no GenAI-specific empirical demonstrations).
3. **paradox-of-expertise** — chose NEW *concept*. Labelled construct, not in KB under any name, distinct from neighbouring constructs (novice-vulnerability, upskilling-deskilling-paradox). Status: emerging (single-source).

The cap on UPDATE proposals: 4 (professional-identity-threat, agency, novice-vulnerability, ai-self-efficacy-erosion). Other plausible candidates considered but excluded to avoid over-linking:
- `metacognitive-laziness` — review's "outsource without mastery" passage maps to the concept but adds nothing the existing entry doesn't already cite. Skipped.
- `fluency-bias` — review's social-presence framing is adjacent but tangential. Skipped.
- `cognitive-debt` — review describes the same pattern in different language; adds no new mechanism. Skipped.
- `ai-loneliness-effect` — review's social-isolation passages support the existing entry but add little beyond a citable peer-reviewed venue. Could be added later if needed; skipped to stay within the 3-5 cap.
