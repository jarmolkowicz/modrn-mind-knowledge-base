## Key Judgment Calls

**System 0 vs. tri-system-theory**: Treated as **sibling frameworks**. Both extend Kahneman's dual-process model to include AI, but they operate on different sides of cognition: Chiriatti et al.'s System 0 is the *input-side substrate* (preprocessor); Shaw & Nave's System 3 is the *output-side parallel agent* (decision partner). The "system" labels (0 vs. 3) come from different authorial choices and are not in tension at the structural level. Created NEW method `system-0-thinking` (the framework deserves its own entry — Heersmink's eight-criterion lens is a distinct analytical apparatus tri-system-theory does not contain) and proposed an UPDATE to tri-system-theory cross-referencing the sibling. This avoids forcing a synthesis the original authors do not endorse while ensuring readers of either entry are pointed to the other.
